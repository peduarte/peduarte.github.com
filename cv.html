Profile

I've always loved the web. I learnt HTML when I was 12 and built a very red website about myself. It was hosted on Geocities.

I was born in Brazil and at the age of 13 (2002), me and my family moved to England. In 2005, I picked up web development again, and by then things had changed slightly. There was this new thing called CSS that I had never heard of.

I then started freelancing and building websites for family, friends and small businesses. I quickly became extremely passionate about design and user experience.

Now I'm currently freelancing. Previously, I was working for one of the top advertising agencies in the world, R/GA, where I built websites and web apps for multi-million pound companies.

I take a lot of pride in my work and I am always eager to improve and learn new technologies. I am a great team player and collaborator with design/ux teams.

My ideal way of working is by developing prototypes as proof of concept and building styleguides in order to keep track of all the modules and make sure the styles are consistent.
